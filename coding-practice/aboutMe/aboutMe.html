Hey Y'all,

My name is Alexandra Schmitt. I am a recent graduate of the Towson University with a Bachelors degree in Computer Science. I currently looking for a job in web development, preferably front end development as that is what I am most familiar with, but I am studying and practicing backend as well. Currently, I am building a website for my cousin. She owns Kim's Butcher Block in Alabama and will be opening a second, larger store near Auburn University. In addition to the website, I will be helping her with her social media as well as with filming a few YouTube videos of her preparing a few meals to showcase her products.

After coming home to Baltimore, I worked as a cake decorator in Pariser's Bakery. Now I bake just for fun. I make a key lime cheesecake that is to die for.

I am a home-body for the most part. I taught myself how to knit, crochet, and quilt. During COVID, when we were all stuck at home, in addition to going to school, I turned the borders of my yard into beautiful gardens full of flowers and veggies. I have one dog. She is a beagle mix and her name is Hershey. I got her when I graduated elementary school, so she is an old lady now.

After traveling and experiencing new things, I want to buy ten-ish arces of land somewhere and start my own homestead. I plan to grow and preserve all my own food and have chickens, ducks, a milk cow, rabbits and maybe even pigs. And a Nova Scotia Duck Tolling Retriever. That's a must!
